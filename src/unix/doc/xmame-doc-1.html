1. Introduction and Links

1.1 Keep everything up-to-date!

The most recent copy of this guide, the xmame-sources and many more can be found on the xmame project homepage at http://x.mame.net/

1.2 Some Links

  • The official M.A.M.E home is at http://www.mame.net
  • The very best ROM site on this planet is at http://www.mame.dk/
  • grustibus, the very good frontend (if you gnome) http://grustibus.sourceforge.net/
  • gnomame, another great gnome-based frontend http://gnomame.sourceforge.net/
  • tkmame, the Tcl/TK frontend that works in Linux/Unix and MS Windows http://tkmame.retrogames.com/
  • MAME Action Replay Page for those who want to have a look at other monitors at http://marp.retrogames.com/
  • and of course don't forget our project homepage at http://x.mame.net/
